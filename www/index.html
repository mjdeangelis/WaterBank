What is WaterBank?

WaterBank allows you to make a simple choice - to drink water. Each time you decide to fill up your water bottle or opt for a water cup when you eat out, log it with the app! You'll be surprised how much money, and calories, you'll end up saving. It's a simple app, for a simple choice.
